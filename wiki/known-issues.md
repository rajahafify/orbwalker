- AR-26 signal capture currently deep-copies `_run_summary` in `RunState._capture_run_signal_state()` and compares full dictionaries in `_emit_run_summary_changed_if_needed(...)`. This preserves correct change detection and is not a current blocker, but it is unnecessary work for ordinary gold/step mutations after a run summary exists. A future cleanup could replace the deep-copy/equality path with a `_run_summary_generation` counter incremented when summaries are finalized, cleared, or restored, while preserving current signal payloads. (source: `scripts/core/run_state.gd`, `docs/architecture_review_tasks.md`)

The evidence showed mixed blockers: first-shop affordability could be weak, low-effectiveness play could die before shop access, and stronger runs still hit level 2-3 survivability pressure. M10-07 closeout compared tuned logs against the untuned baseline and found no Milestone 10 blocker: `run_1777968781_770133_2026-05-05t16_13_01` died in Dungeon 3, `run_1777969048_434533_2026-05-05t16_17_28` won a full run, and `run_1777973747_694854_2026-05-05t17_35_47` reached L3 enemy 2 after first-shop affordability, booster buys, equipment buys, and two boss relic rewards.
