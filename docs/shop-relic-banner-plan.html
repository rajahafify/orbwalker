Port Back Rules

  • The banner frame must be its own horizontal asset, not a resized collection card.
  • Remove decorative ornaments from this banner and future matching shop frames. No center clasp, no corner flourishes, no extra hardware. Keep the frame to bevel lines, texture, and readable content lanes.
  • Content starts below the rail. Target padding is 31px top and 29px bottom inside a 178px banner.
  • Relic art is capped for spiky silhouettes. The stage is 184x118; the image is capped to 112x90.
  • The art stage uses a soft dark/purple well without its own border. The banner frame is already the container.
  • Title, tier, copy, and price are separate lanes. No text shares the top frame band.
  • The price badge is a simple padded asset with no side ornaments. The old ornate badge reads clipped in small shop scale and should be removed from this banner style.
  • Unavailable relics keep the real price, for example $24, and dim the price badge visually.
