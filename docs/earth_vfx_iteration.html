Earth Combat VFX Iteration

Documented May 23, 2026. This captures the current accepted Earth post-match VFX direction after separating spool, travel, and impact layers.

Accepted Direction

Earth is now built as separate readable phases instead of one reused effect scaled up and down:

  • Spool uses a stable green-tinted tornado and ground buildup at the mastery card.
  • Travel uses the green fractal/caustics lane as the crawling earth-crack movement toward the enemy.
  • Impact uses dust, splash, bubbles, shock rings, stone chunks, and pack-hit bursts rather than the travel fractal.
  • Strong Earth adds the atmospheric tornado only inside the enemy target area, with extra debris and quake layers.

Implementation Notes

  • Earth now has dedicated Max VFX replay, cast, spool, travel, impact, and tornado-atmosphere helpers in CombatMaxVfxOverlay.
  • The Alenia atmospheric caustics sheet is reserved for Earth travel/fracture only.
  • Earth impact now routes to rain_splash and bubbles as atmospheric impact/secondary keys, with stone and dust flipbooks layered over them.
  • The tornado scene is pinned for spool use, so it no longer drifts across the board during the charge phase.
  • The Earth palette was shifted greener while preserving brown dust and stone chunks for impact readability.

Debug Gallery

The VFX gallery remains the preferred place to compare Earth amount scaling.

  • res://scenes/debug/vfx_gallery_show.tscn can play Earth full sequence, travel, impact, and label phases.
  • Use amount presets 4, 12, 25, and 50 to compare weak impact, medium buildup, and strong target-area tornado behavior.
  • Watch specifically that the fractal appears during travel, not during impact.
