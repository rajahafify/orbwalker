Alenia Studios - 20 Pixel Art Atmospheric VFX Pack
Hi! I'm KXLT, the indie developer behind Alenia Studios.

This atmospheric visual effects (VFX) pack is the result of my personal exploration into the world of pixel art. While I am not a professional artist, this pack was carefully designed with a technical approach to achieve the best possible immersion in 2D environments.

To create and polish these assets, I didn't use standard commercial animation software. The entire pack was developed and rendered using our own custom internal toolset—the Alenia Ecosystem—utilizing dedicated viewers like PixelView and processors like Alenia GifPreview to ensure seamless loops and pixel-perfect shading.

📂 Pack Contents
Includes 20 climatic and atmospheric effects, all featuring a perfect 48-frame loop, ready to be integrated into engines like Godot, Ren'Py, or any 2D engine you prefer.

1. Cozy Snow: Soft snowflakes falling slowly.
2. Aesthetic Rain: Heavy rain with a crunchy alpha trail effect.
3. Aesthetic Wind: Fast gusts to add dynamism to your environments.
4. Autumn Leaves: Orange leaves with an organic, natural wobble.
5. Epic Tornado: A massive funnel with directional shading and realistic volume.
6. Cozy Spores (Fireflies): Golden particles that float upwards and pulse softly.
7. Fire Embers / Ashes: Sparks that ascend with the heat and cool down (change color) mid-air.
8. God Rays: Subtle, natural volumetric lighting to illuminate backgrounds.
9. Meteor Shower: Shooting stars that dash quickly across the night sky.
10. Sakura Petals: Smooth, curved falling cherry blossoms in pastel tones.
11. Swamp Bubbles: Toxic green bubbles that float upwards from the ground.
12. Boiling Bubbles: Fast-paced, chaotic bubbles with an explosive popping effect.
13. Cinematic Snow: Complex geometric ice crystals with realistic fluttering physics.
14. Ground Rain & Splashes: Raindrops that hit the floor, creating dynamic ripples and splash particles.
15. Fractal Lightning Storm: High-voltage procedural branching lightning strikes.
16. Magic Wind & Fairy Dust: Sparkling magical trails swirling in complex loops.
17. Underwater Caustics: Organic, Voronoi-based light refraction patterns for swimming pools or oceans.
18. Creeping Frost: Ice crystals that slowly freeze the screen starting from the edges.
19. Aurora Borealis: Multi-layered, waving neon curtains of light in the night sky.
20. Phantom Fog of War: Dense, horizontal scrolling retro fog that fills the screen (GBA RPG style).

⚙️ Technical Specifications
Base Resolution: 320x180 px (Easily scalable for any retro project).
Frames: 48 frames per animation (Seamless Loop).
Included Formats: 
* Full spritesheets (.png).
* Folders containing individual frame sequences for maximum control.
